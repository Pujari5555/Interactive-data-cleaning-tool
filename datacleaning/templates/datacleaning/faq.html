Frequently Asked Questions

Question 1: How do I change my password?

Answer: Go to settings and click on 'Change Password'.

Question 2: How do I enable notifications?

Answer: Use the toggle switch in the settings tab to enable notifications.

Question 3: How can I update my profile picture?

Answer: Go to settings, and there will be an option to upload or change your profile picture.

Question 4: How do I change the theme of the website (Light/Dark mode)?

Answer: Use the toggle switch in the settings tab to switch between light and dark mode.

Question 5: Can I delete my account?

Answer: Yes, you can delete your account by contacting customer support from the settings tab.

Question 6: How do I log out of my account?

Answer: You can log out by clicking the 'Logout' button in the profile section.

Question 7: I forgot my password, what should I do?

Answer: Click on 'Forgot Password' on the login page to reset your password via email.

Question 8: How do I contact customer support?

Answer: You can contact customer support by visiting the 'Help' section in the settings tab.
